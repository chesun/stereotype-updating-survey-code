In the next part of the experiment, you will face a series of questions in which you will be asked to choose between a list of uncertain options. Specifically, on every line, you will be asked to choose between:

  • Option A: Earn $4 if the score of the top odd-ID person is higher than the top even-ID person in the group.
  • Option B: Earn $4 if the ball randomly drawn from Basket B is smaller than or equal to a certain number.

Basket B contains 100 balls numbered 1 to 100. Each number appears in the basket exactly once and all balls are equal likely to be drawn.
