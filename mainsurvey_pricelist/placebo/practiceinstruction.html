In this practice question, you will be asked to choose between a list of uncertain options. Specifically, on every line, you will be asked to choose between:

  • Option A: Earn $4 with 45% chance.
  • Option B: Earn $4 if the ball randomly drawn from Basket B (see below) is smaller than or equal to a certain number.

Basket B contains 100 balls numbered 1 to 100. Each number appears in the basket exactly once and all balls are equal likely to be drawn.
